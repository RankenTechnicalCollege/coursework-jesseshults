Tacos

This is a basic recipe for homemade tacos.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (or chicken)
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 small taco shells
  • 1 package of tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • Salsa (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef (or chicken) in a skillet over medium heat until browned.
  2. Drain excess fat and stir in taco seasoning with a little water. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Warm taco shells according to package instructions.
  4. Fill each taco shell with seasoned meat.
  5. Top with lettuce, cheese, tomatoes, onions, and any other desired toppings.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
